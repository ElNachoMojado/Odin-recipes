Neapolitan Flan

Description

A recipe for a mean neapolitan flan. Mexicans can't take the credit for this but we eat it very often and it is the best thing to have after some street tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 can condensed milk (if you have access to La Lechera, use that).
  • 1 can evaporated milk (if you have access to Carnation, go with THAT).
  • 5 eggs.
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla essence.
  • Half a cup of caster sugar.

Steps

  1. Add sugar to a pot and caramelize. Once it's able to flow, let it cool.
  2. Blend the rest of the ingredients and add mix to the pot with the caramel.
  3. Preheat oven at 180 degrees celsius.
  4. Set up a water bath for the flan and put it in the oven. Bake for an hour.
